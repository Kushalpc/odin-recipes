Momo

Description

Momo is a type of steamed dumpling popular in Nepal, Tibet, and parts of India, typically filled with vegetables and/or meat and served with a spicy dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1 cup finely chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1/2 pound ground meat (chicken, pork, or beef)

Procedure

  1. Prepare Dough: Mix flour with water to form a smooth dough. Knead well and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare Filling: In a bowl, combine chopped vegetables, ground meat, and season with salt, pepper, and optional soy sauce.
  3. Roll and Fill: Divide the dough into small balls, roll each into thin circles. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each circle.
  4. Shape Momos: Fold and pleat the edges to seal the filling inside.
  5. Steam: Place momos in a steamer and cook for 10-15 minutes, until the dough is cooked through. Serve hot with dipping sauce.
